Let Us Be Your Partner In Energy Storage

Mesho Battery is a India-based long duration energy storage solutions provider manufacturing low-cost and efficient modular vanadium redox flow batteries. Mesho Battery’s long-term vision is to drive the world towards energy equity where everyone can access clean energy at affordable pricing. With a 25-year expected lifespan, Mesho Battery has one of the safest and most environmentally friendly battery technologies. Mesho Battery, batteries can store renewable energy over long-duration and are designed to address issues of performance degradation, thermal runaway, and product reliability on safety.
